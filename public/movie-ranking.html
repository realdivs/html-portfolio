Best Movies according to Divyanshi

My top 3 movies of all-time

3 Idiots

I have seen this movie almost 20-30 times and if you ask me to watch it again then also I will be ready to watch it. It never gets old that is the magic of this movie. It always leaves a smile on my face.
Year : 2009

Jab We Met

This movie is very close to my heart beacuse it has the love-story that we all want to manifest in our real-life. Kareena's performance in this movie is just OP and she gives a lot of expressions and is very much into the role of 'GEET'. P.S. : She is one of my favourite heroines as well.
Year : 2007

Welcome

When I was a kid I found this movie very much funny as this movie is surely GOAT(Greatest Of All Time). This movie is a masterpiece. Akshay Kumar's acting was top-level in this movie as a main lead in the movie.
Year : 2007
